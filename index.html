Discovering Health in Your Own Kitchen

Traditional Filipino Healing & Wellness

Chichi Barba is a traditional Filipino healing advocate specializing in traditional Filipino healing and nutritional wellness. She guides individuals toward simple, sustainable lifestyle changes through the healing power of food.

As an author of multiple books on food and wellness, Chichi shares simple Filipino health practices that can help fight illnesses and promote better health. Her work bridges ancient wisdom with modern wellness, making traditional healing accessible to everyone.

She is a SHEM (Self-Health Empowerment Movement) practitioner and occasionally joins Dr. Romy Paredes in supporting individuals ready to embark on their own healing and transformation. She also volunteers with NGOs advocating for sustainable agriculture and food sovereignty.

Chichi has trained under renowned Filipino albularyo Bibiano Fajardo, PhD (AM) in Traditional Filipino Healing through the Association of Traditional Health Aid Givers, Inc. (ATHAG Inc.). She is also a certified Neuro-Linguistic Programming Master Practitioner through the Lead More Development Center under Jojo Apolo.

Living in Manila, Philippines, Chichi maintains a career in IT, freelances as a writer, and cares for her family. In her free time, she enjoys exploring local markets for native ingredients and promoting food sovereignty.
